Eligibility

Candidates with at least 50% of marks either in Bachelor's Degree and / or in the Master's Degree or any other equivalent qualification are eligible for admission.

SUBJECT OFFERING

Candidates have to opt any one method subjects from the following:-

  1. Language (English/Bengali)
  2. Mathematics
  3. Science (Physical science/Biological Sciences)
  4. Social Sciences (History/Geography/Education)

AGE LIMIT

Candidates should be below 35 years on 1st July of every academic year for the freshers. SC, ST, PH candidates will get the opportunity of age relaxation.

Marks Distribution In Different Qualification , For Admission to B.ED. Course (According To WBSU Circular): -

Academic Performance Marks
Ph.D. / D.Phil 5
M.Phil 3
Post Graduate M.A. / M.Sc. / M.Com. 35
Degree (With Hons.) 30
Degree (With Spl. Hons.) 20
Degree (With Pass / General) 10
H.S. (+2) or Equivalent 20
Madhyamic or Equivalent 10

N.B. During Interview candidates must have to show all relevant papers in original which they have submitted with the application.

Selection will be finally decided by the college authority.
